About this site

Read about, review and comment on applications for use of Crown land in British Columbia.

About this site

Section 32(2) of the Land Act sets out the Province’s requirement to publish Crown land applications on a publicly accessibly website. Public participation in the administration of Crown land is encouraged and this site offers a platform for members of the public to submit comments for consideration.

This site contains Crown land application information posted by the Province as of October 15, 2018. Once an application has been reviewed and a decision made, it will remain on the site for a limited period of time.

For Crown land application information posted prior to October 15, 2018, please refer to the Applications and Reasons for Decision (ARFD) website (listed below).

Application Review Status

Applications progress through potentially two phases: application review and decision.

Application Review

  1. Application Under Review: The application is received by the Province for consideration and posted on ACRFD.
  2. Application Review Complete - Decision Pending: The consultation period of review is complete and the Province is making its final decision.

Decision

  1. Abandoned: The application review and processing has been cancelled or abandoned (by either the client or applicant). No further action will be taken.
  2. Approved - Tenure Issued: The application review has concluded and a determination has been made to approve the application. All review processes are complete at this stage.
  3. Not Approved: The application review has concluded and it has been determined that the application will not be approved, and will not receive a tenure.
