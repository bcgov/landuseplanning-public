About this Site

This website is used to post Crown Land Application information for public review and comment, as per Section 32(2) of the Land Act, and to support Government's mandate to be transparent in making decisions related to Crown Land Applications.

The website has been launched as a pilot for the Province's Skeena Region, and will be continuously improved and rolled out provincially over the next year. We are always looking for feedback on the PRC website. To share your thoughts, email us at NRTS.PRC@gov.bc.ca.

If you wish to view and comment on Crown Land Applications in other regions of the Province, please visit arfd.gov.bc.ca
